1. Jesse Livermore - Pivotal Points & Money Management

**Jesse Livermore** is legendary. He shorted the 1929 crash making $100M. His core trading focused on price trends, **Pivotal Points**, and strict capital rules.

Core Trading Principles:

  • Pivotal Point Breakout：Do not trade range markets. Enter only when price breaks historical support/resistance levels. This signals a new trend.
  • Testing Entry (Pyramiding)：Never buy all at once. Build a 20% pilot position, and add more only if the trade shows a profit to prove you are correct.
  • Cut Losses, Let Profits Run：Livermore pioneered the 10% hard stop-loss. If a position drops 10%, exit unconditionally.

2. Richard Dennis - The Turtle Trading System

In 1983, commodities master **Richard Dennis** recruited novices ("Turtles") and taught them a systematic trend-following strategy in two weeks. They generated tens of millions, proving trading can be taught.

Turtle Trading Rules:

  • Markets Selection：Trade only highly liquid and volatile markets (commodities futures, FX, bonds).
  • 唐奇安通道突破 (Donchian Channel)：
    • System 1 (Short-term): Enter on a 20-day high breakout; exit on a 10-day low breakout.
    • System 2 (Long-term): Enter on a 55-day high breakout; exit on a 20-day low breakout.
  • 基於波動率的頭寸管理 (N 值 / ATR)：Use Average True Range (ATR) to calculate position size, normalizing volatility risk across different markets.

3. George Soros - Macro Trading & Reflexivity

George Soros is famous for "breaking the Bank of England" in 1992. His strategy is built on his philosophical model: **Reflexivity**.

Core of Reflexivity Theory:

Traditional economics assumes market rationality. Soros argues that **participants' biases change the market fundamentals, which shifts prices, which in turn reinforces biases.** It is a circular feedback loop.

Soros's Bubble Cycle:
Bias ➡️ Price Rises ➡️ Fundamentals Distorted ➡️ Exponential Price Boom (Bubble) ➡️ Bias Popped ➡️ Market Crashes.

Soros's strategy: **Find inflection points where biases diverge severely from reality, ride the bubble, and execute massive counter-attacks near the popping point.**
